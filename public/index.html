Admission Requirements

To ensure a smooth enrollment process, please prepare the following original and photocopied documents.

Original Form 138 (Report Card)

Must be signed by the high school principal. For transferees, submit your latest True Copy of Grades.

Original Form 137 / Official Transcript

Permanent student academic record. Can be processed as "To Follow" if currently unavailable.

Certificate of Good Moral Character

Issued by the last school attended, dated within the last 6 months.

PSA Birth Certificate

1 Original and 2 Clear Photocopies.

Recent 2x2 ID Pictures

3 pieces, white background with name tag.
